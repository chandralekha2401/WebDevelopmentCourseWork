About us

We are a rat friendly typical french dining experience founded in 2007 by Remy. Remy's key inspiration was the famed chef Augste Gusteau -who's motto "Anyone can cook!" propelled Remy's culinary exploration. Furthermore, his extraordinary sence of smell enabled him to tastefully combine flavours together in a most unique manner. Here today at Gusteau's we continue with some of the very dishes which make Remy the rat he is today. One of these being the most famous ratatouille!
